Key Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

Does the network evaluation require halting our operations?
No. Our initial diagnostics and audits are performed using passive, read-only methods. Your operations, users, and critical systems will not experience any interruption during data collection.
We have hardware from different brands, can you integrate it?
Absolutely. We are brand agnostic and specialists in multivendor environments (Cisco, Huawei, Fortinet, Aruba, etc.). Our approach is to optimize the performance of the hardware you already own before suggesting new investments (CAPEX).
How do you differ from our ISP or internal support team?
Your internal team and Internet Service Provider maintain daily operations (Helpdesk). We act as Level 3 strategic consultants. We step in to resolve complex bottlenecks, design network reengineering, segment for security (VLANs/Hardening), and leave a solid infrastructure that your internal team can easily manage.
Our company is not in CDMX or Morelos, do you have coverage?
Yes. Although our main physical base is in CDMX and Morelos, we design architectures, complex remote configurations (VPN, SD-WAN, Proxmox), and manage national and international projects for the corporate, industrial, and hotel sectors.
