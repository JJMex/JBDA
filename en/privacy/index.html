Comprehensive Privacy Policy

At JBDA | Strategic Network Infrastructure Consulting, we are committed to protecting the personal data of our clients, prospects, and business partners. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about the treatment that will be given to your personal data when it is collected, used, stored, and/or transferred by us, in compliance with the Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP) of Mexico.

1. Identity and Address of the Data Controller

JBDA (hereinafter "the Firm"), with principal operations in Mexico City and the State of Morelos, is responsible for the use and protection of your personal data. For any questions or comments regarding this policy, you may contact us via our corporate email: JBDA_Tech@icloud.com.

2. Personal Data Collected

To carry out the purposes described in this privacy policy, we will collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identification and contact data: Full name, job title or position, company you work for, institutional or mobile phone number, and corporate email.
  • Technical and infrastructure data: Information related to network topologies, hardware inventory (switches, firewalls, routers), VLAN configurations, internal IP addresses, and any other technical data strictly necessary for the execution of audits or stress simulations. Note: JBDA assumes a role of "Processor" or consultant of this technical information, which is treated under the strictest standards of confidentiality and professional ethics.

JBDA does not collect sensitive personal data (such as racial origin, health status, religious beliefs, or political affiliation) through its website or during the initial consulting process.

3. Purposes of Data Processing

The personal data we collect from you will be used for the following primary purposes, which are necessary for the service you request:

  • To provide the strategic consulting, auditing, and network reengineering services requested from us.
  • To generate technical and commercial proposals, and quotations (CAPEX optimization).
  • To establish contact and follow-up for scheduling preliminary diagnostic sessions through third-party platforms (such as Calendly).
  • To provide technical support and executive attention through our support portal.
  • To prepare contracts, non-disclosure agreements (NDAs), and billing corresponding to the contracted services.

4. Data Transfers

JBDA informs you that your contact personal data will not be sold, rented, or transferred to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your data may only be transferred or shared inside or outside the country with:

  • Hardware manufacturers (e.g., Cisco, Fortinet, Huawei) only in cases where it is strictly necessary for the registration of licenses for equipment purchased by the client, warranty management (RMA), or escalation of advanced technical support, always with the prior consent and knowledge of the client.
  • Competent authorities in the cases provided by law.

5. Security Measures

To protect your personal data against damage, loss, alteration, destruction, or unauthorized use, access, or processing, JBDA has established and maintains administrative, technical, and physical security measures, aligned with the best industrial cybersecurity and infrastructure hardening practices that we implement in our clients.

6. ARCO Rights

You have the right to know what personal data we have about you, what we use it for, and the conditions of the use we give it (Access). Furthermore, it is your right to request the correction of your personal information in case it is outdated, inaccurate, or incomplete (Rectification); that we remove it from our records or databases (Cancellation); as well as to oppose the use of your personal data for specific purposes (Opposition). These rights are known as ARCO rights.

To exercise any of the ARCO rights, you must submit the respective request by sending an email to our privacy team at JBDA_Tech@icloud.com.
